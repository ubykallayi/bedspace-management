User Roles & Access

Clear RBAC structure makes the platform suitable for operational teams, property owners, and residents.

Secure By Role
Super Admin

Full platform control

Manages settings, users, property creation, role assignment, backups, restore, and full operational visibility.

Owner

Property-level command

Full management access for assigned properties, including rooms, tenants, payments, and expenses.

Manager

Operational execution

Handles tenants, rent collection, manual charges, expenses, and day-to-day performance without property administration power.

Tenant

Self-service visibility

Views only personal bookings, payment status, receipts, and booking lifecycle details.

Highlights That Differentiate This App

These are the features that make the product feel tailored to real bedspace operations instead of generic property software.

High-Impact Features
1

Advanced Bookings

Future tenants can be reserved into beds while the current tenant remains active until the scheduled handover point.

2

Prorated Rent

The first cycle can automatically calculate prorated rent from the tenant start date and carry that into balance logic.

3

Manual Charges

Opening balances, damages, and special recoveries stay separate from payments while still affecting collection totals correctly.

4

Drive Backup & Restore

Google Drive backup, restore from file, and restore-from-Drive support make the platform operationally resilient.
